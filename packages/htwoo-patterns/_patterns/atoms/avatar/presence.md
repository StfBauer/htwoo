---
title: Presence
---

This prescence bubble is used to indicate a persons availability status expecially on avatars. The following status are defined:

* away - Away
* dnd - Do not disturbe
* online - Online
* invisible - Invisible
* off - Out of Office

For visual appearance check out [Presence All](/?p=atoms-presence-all),
